Study Tips

  • Time Management: Make a schedule and study regularly.
  • Note Taking: Use headings and summarize important ideas.
  • Avoid Distractions: Keep your phone away while studying.
  • Practice: Review notes and answer quizzes.
  • Stay Healthy: Sleep well and eat properly.
